About Us

Mission

Beginning with one store in Cavendish Square. Claremont, we’ve expanded to 3 locations in Cape Town, expanding our clientele to more Cape Town residents. Our priority is to provide a comfortable, inspiring shopping experience that is user-friendly and accessible.

Our Customers

Our products and services cater to a broad range of audiences, including but not limited to:

  • Homeowners looking to decorate or furnish their homes
  • Renters looking for temporary or portable furniture and decor options
  • Interior designers and decorators
  • Housewarming and wedding gift shoppers
  • Small business owners looking to decorate their office spaces
  • Architects and builders seeking high-quality and unique home goods for projects
  • DIY enthusiasts and hobbyists looking for inspiration and supplies for home improvement projects
  • Individuals interested in sustainable or eco-friendly home goods options
  • Budget-conscious shoppers seeking affordable but stylish home goods options
  • Luxury and high-end home goods shoppers
Our Services

We’re passionate about making our customers happy. From online browsing to in-person shopping, you can always count on a Home Goods associate to support your customer experience. Our associates are well-trained and experienced to handle all customer inquiries and exceed your expectations.
